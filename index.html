Hi! I'm Brian.

An entrepreneur and business analytics
professional in NYC.

I'm originally from Davis, CA and I graduated from UC Berkeley (go bears!) with a B.A. in Statistics with an emphasis in Computer Science and a B.S. in Business from the Haas School. Out of Cal I've spent the past 3 years working on an ed-tech startup, Leada. I love the process of having an idea and working my tail off to see it come to life.
